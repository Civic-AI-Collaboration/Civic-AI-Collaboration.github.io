About

CIVIC-AI is a workshop bringing together researchers to examine the interplay between artificial intelligence, work, and society.

Our central questions:

  • Future of Work & Automation Which professions and tasks are being automated away, and which are being augmented? What does the evidence tell us about the real shape of displacement — and who bears its costs?
  • Human Advantage What constitutes irreducible human value in a world where a large fraction of knowledge work can be automated? How do we define, measure, and support it?
  • Cross-cultural Dimensions How do cultural context, economic development, and policy environment shape AI adoption, usage norms, and the lived experience of automation across different societies?
